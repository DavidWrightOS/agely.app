Ages

Every age, always up to date.

The Ages tab is a simple list of your people and how old they are today — no counting back from birth years.

  • See ages down to the year, month, and day.
  • Filter by group when you only need family or friends.
  • Tap anyone to see how much older or younger everyone else is.

Upcoming

Always know whose birthday is next.

The Upcoming tab lays out the months ahead, so you can see who needs a card, a call, or a gift — with time to spare.

  • Today's birthdays stand out the moment you open the app.
  • Birthdays are grouped by month, so planning ahead is easy.
  • Countdowns show exactly how long you have to get ready.

Calculator

How old was everyone in that photo?

The Calculator answers questions like that in seconds — from when your daughter was exactly 18 months old, to how old everyone will be in 2030.

  • Start from a date or an age, whichever you know.
  • Go back to any date, or ahead to one that hasn't happened yet.
  • Get the answer for everyone at once.

Group filtering

Focus on one group at a time.

Tap a group to filter your list, then flip between current ages and upcoming birthdays whenever you need a different view.

Reminders

Stay on top of birthdays with helpful reminders.

Get a reminder for every birthday, or only for the people you choose — and decide when the heads-up arrives, from the day itself to three weeks ahead.

Privacy

Your data stays yours.

Names, birthdays, notes, groups, and photos stay on your device, or in your private iCloud account. Nothing you save is ever sold, shared for advertising, or used to track you.
