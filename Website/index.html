Ages

Every age, instantly clear.

Agely turns birthdays into a simple list of people and ages, so you can stop recomputing dates in your head.

  • Sort and scan ages quickly.
  • Tap a group filter to focus on the people you need.
  • Keep helpful notes with each person.

Upcoming

Always know whose birthday is next.

The Upcoming tab makes birthdays feel manageable. Scan the next few weeks or months and know who needs a card, a call, or a gift.

  • Today's birthdays stand out the moment you open the app.
  • Birthdays are grouped by month, so planning ahead is easy.
  • Countdowns show exactly how long you have to get ready.

Calculator

A birthday calculator that lives with your people.

Calculate age, birthdate, and date spans from the same place you already keep birthdays. It is quick enough for a conversation and organized enough for real planning.

  • Switch between age and date inputs.
  • See everyone's age on any date, past or future.
  • Enter an age like 18 and see the exact date each person reaches it.

Private by design

Your personal Agely data stays personal.

Names, birthdays, notes, groups, and photos stay on your device, or in your private iCloud account if you turn on sync. Nothing you save is ever sold, shared for advertising, or used to track you.
