For the clearest bug report

  • What you were trying to do.
  • What happened instead.
  • Whether it happens every time.
  • Your app version, iOS version, and device model.

For privacy requests

Please avoid sending contact names, birthdays, photos, notes, Apple ID, iCloud email address, or other app content unless it is needed for your request.

Privacy requests can be sent directly to privacy@agely.app, and the Privacy Policy explains how Agely handles app data, analytics, and iCloud sync.

Wondering about Diagnostics IDs? Agely does not have any. Analytics and diagnostics are never linked to you, and they never include the people, birthdays, or notes you save in Agely.
